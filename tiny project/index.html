6.4 Query Source Topic Relevance

High

The main theme of the webpage content is the intended topic of the query. If the title of the webpage is closely related to the query, it is usually a strong indicator. Also, the pages on the website are dedicated to topics of the query. Only in the case of wikipedia.org, if the webpage is relevant to the query, please mark the source as “high” even though the entire Wikipedia content is not focused on a single theme.

Partial

Please grade it as “Partial” if the webpage content is a good source for the query, but not the website. The main theme of the content is the intended topic of the query. However, the website is primarily focused on topics not related to the query, or it caters to a broader range of topics, e.g., Q&A sites.

Unrelated

The main theme of the webpage content is unrelated to the intended topic of the query, e.g. title or the beginning text on the page is not related to the query topic.
